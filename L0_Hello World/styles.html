We want to make our text fancier!

But we have to use CSS to customize our text!
Use the style attribute to set your style.
Colons are used to declare a style, and semicolons separate styling. Just remember to not confuse colons with equals sign, and watch out for quotes! There are a variety of styles you can use. They work great in an IDE! The possibilities are endless!

The syntax for a style is <tagname style="property:value"> !
Use the div or span as the generic tagname
Preformatted text are immune to styles
